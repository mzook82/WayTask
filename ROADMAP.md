# WayTask Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the long-term vision for WayTask.
It is intended to guide product development and communicate the direction of the project.
Features and priorities may change based on beta feedback and user needs.

---

# Vision

WayTask aims to become the smartest shopping assistant by combining AI, location awareness, and real-time store information to simplify everyday shopping.

---

# Success Metrics

WayTask aims to achieve:

- Reliable shopping experience
- Accurate nearby store recommendations
- Fast AI product recognition
- High beta tester satisfaction
- Stable weekly beta releases

---

# Guiding Principles

Every new feature should follow these principles:

- Keep the user experience simple.
- Solve real shopping problems.
- Prioritize quality over quantity.
- Release small, stable improvements.
- Listen to beta tester feedback.


# Out of Scope

The following ideas are intentionally not planned at this stage:

- Grocery delivery
- In-app payments
- Marketplace functionality
- Social shopping feed
