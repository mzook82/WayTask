# Audit Result

## Outcome

The audit successfully identified the missing integration points of the Shopping Flow.

Most recommendations have been implemented in Beta 1.0.1.

## Implemented

- Store Selection
- Shopping Session integration
- Active Shopping Mode
- Apple Maps handoff
- Resume Shopping
- Resume after relaunch
- Finish Shopping

## Deferred

- Shopping List Selector UX
- Product Row UX refinement
- Store Coverage Presentation
- Community Store Intelligence

## Lessons Learned

The Shopping Flow architecture was largely complete before implementation.

The primary work involved integrating existing components rather than redesigning the architecture.

This audit confirmed that structured analysis before implementation significantly reduced development risk and unnecessary refactoring.

## Final Audit Result

Shopping Flow implementation completed successfully.

Implemented:

- Shopping Session Integration
- Store Selection
- Resume Shopping
- Resume After Relaunch
- Product Row UX
- Honest Store Coverage

Deferred:

- Home Experience
- First-Time Guidance
- Community Intelligence

Conclusion:

The Shopping Flow architecture required integration rather than redesign.

The audit-driven workflow significantly reduced unnecessary refactoring.
