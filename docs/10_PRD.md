# 1. Executive Summary

WayTask is an AI-powered location-aware shopping companion for iOS.

Unlike traditional shopping list applications, WayTask actively assists users before, during, and after shopping by combining location awareness, intelligent shopping lists, interactive maps, computer vision, and artificial intelligence.

The application continuously helps users discover nearby opportunities to purchase products they already planned to buy while also recommending new stores, restaurants, products, and personalized suggestions.

WayTask is designed to transform shopping into a proactive, intelligent, and enjoyable experience.

---

# 2. Vision

Shopping should be effortless.

People should never have to remember where they wanted to buy something, forget products they needed, or miss opportunities while passing near relevant stores.

WayTask becomes a personal shopping companion that quietly works in the background and delivers helpful information exactly when it becomes relevant.

The long-term vision is to combine Artificial Intelligence, Location Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Personalized Recommendations into a single intelligent shopping platform.

---

# 3. Problem Statement

Traditional shopping list applications have several limitations:

- They only store information.
- They never actively help users.
- They don't understand the user's location.
- They don't recognize products.
- They don't recommend nearby opportunities.
- They don't learn shopping habits.
- They don't assist with decision making.

As a result, users often:

- Forget products.
- Visit multiple stores unnecessarily.
- Miss nearby shopping opportunities.
- Waste time.
- Purchase inefficiently.

WayTask solves these problems by proactively assisting users throughout the shopping journey.

---

# 4. Product Philosophy

WayTask follows several core principles.

## 4.1 Proactive, not Reactive

The application should proactively assist users instead of waiting for them to search manually.

---

## 4.2 Context Matters

Recommendations should always consider:

- Current location
- Time of day
- User interests
- Shopping history
- Active shopping lists

---

## 4.3 Simplicity

Every interaction should require as few taps as possible.

The interface should feel clean, modern, and unmistakably native to iOS.

---

## 4.4 Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence should quietly enhance the experience without overwhelming users.

Recommendations should feel natural rather than intrusive.

---

## 4.5 Privacy

User privacy is fundamental.

Location, camera usage, shopping history, and AI features should respect Apple's privacy principles and give users full control over their data.

---

# 5. Product Goals

WayTask aims to:

- Reduce forgotten purchases.
- Save users time.
- Improve shopping efficiency.
- Help users discover relevant nearby places.
- Personalize shopping recommendations.
- Simplify product discovery.
- Build an intelligent shopping experience powered by AI.
