# WayTask Future Ideas

> This document contains long-term ideas and vision concepts for future versions of WayTask.
>
> These ideas are intentionally separated from the MVP roadmap.
> They are not scheduled for implementation, but they help define the long-term direction of the product.

---

# AI Environment Scan

## Vision

Instead of only understanding what the user wants to buy, WayTask will understand what the user already owns.

The goal is to bridge the gap between the physical world and shopping decisions.

---

# Smart Scan Session

Instead of taking a single photo, users start an AI-powered scan session.

Flow:

Start Scan

↓

Move camera around the environment

↓

WayTask builds a complete understanding of the scanned area

↓

Finish Scan

↓

Summary

The app analyzes all detected objects and presents useful actions.

# Long-Term Vision

WayTask evolves from a shopping application into an intelligent personal lifestyle assistant.

Instead of only answering:

"What should I buy?"

WayTask will also understand:

- What do I already own?
- What am I running out of?
- What can I make?
- What should I buy next?
- Where should I buy it?
- When should I buy it?
- What should I avoid buying again?

The ultimate goal is to help users make smarter purchasing decisions while reducing waste, saving time, and saving money.
