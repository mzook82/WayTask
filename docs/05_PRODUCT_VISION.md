# Mission

WayTask exists to make shopping effortless.

By combining location awareness, artificial intelligence, and computer vision, WayTask helps people make smarter shopping decisions at exactly the right place and the right time.

---

# Vision

We believe shopping should become proactive instead of reactive.

Rather than relying on memory, users should receive intelligent assistance before they even realize they need it.

WayTask becomes a trusted shopping companion that quietly works in the background, delivering timely recommendations without interrupting everyday life.

WayTask should never feel like an application. It should feel like a personal shopping companion.

---

# Long-Term Vision

WayTask is not just a shopping list application.

It is the foundation of an intelligent shopping platform that combines:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Location Intelligence
- Computer Vision
- Personalized Recommendations
- Smart Navigation
- Calendar Awareness
- Shopping Automation

Our long-term goal is to build the most intelligent shopping assistant available on iPhone.

---

# Product Principles

## Proactive

WayTask should anticipate user needs instead of waiting for manual interaction.

---

## Helpful

Every notification should provide genuine value.

If a notification does not help the user, it should not be sent.

---

## Intelligent

Artificial Intelligence should simplify decisions rather than complicate them.

AI exists to assist users—not replace them.

---

## Privacy First

User privacy is fundamental.

Personal data, shopping habits, location history, and AI features should always remain under the user's control.

WayTask follows Apple's privacy-first philosophy.

---

## Native Experience

WayTask should feel like a natural part of iOS.

The interface should be elegant, intuitive, fast, and consistent with Apple's Human Interface Guidelines.
